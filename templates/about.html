About Eastern Logistics

Our Story

Eastern Logistics is a professional courier and transportation company serving Toronto, the GTA, and surrounding areas.

We noticed that businesses in the Greater Toronto Area needed a reliable, flexible delivery partner that could handle everything from small parcels to furniture, without the complexity and high minimums of national carriers.

Starting with just two sprinter vans, we've grown to a diverse fleet of vehicles serving hundreds of businesses across the GTA. Our focus remains the same: provide exceptional service, reliable delivery, and personalized attention to every client.
